Introduction to Cookies This is the site of your dreams. Have you ever looked up how many cookies there are on google? I just checked and there seems to be around 130 of them. And whenever you are around a place with a variety of cookies, there could be a couple to a couple dozens. It’s a struggle because cookies have a lot of calories, you can’t just try them all, that will ruin your diet. Also, its bad food edict, people will think you’re a cookie monster or something. But here you are, I will try to come up with a plan to figure out which cookies to take which the opportunity presents itself to you. Because everyone has been in that situation, where you go to a party, banquet, potluck, whatever you want to call it. It has caught us all of guard and then we don’t know what to take. It’s the same thing as when you try to choose Mary Ann’s Ice cream, Sea’s candies chocolate, or anything else like that. What Site Does? Well, how? Break it down all into a deep deep science that can quantity which one you would like better. That seems like a good idea. One thing to keep in mind in this exercise is that when the opportunity does present to you, realistically there are probably only about 3-6 cookies max in variety. So, it would be such a complicated process as if all 130 types of cookies are all on a dinner table. Your choices are between 6 perhaps, and that’s easier.
